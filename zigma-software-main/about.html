We, Zigma Software Consultancy, are a professional Organization comprising a set of dedicated software professionals around 10 plus, ever striving towards perfection from 1991 onwards. Doing Tailor-made software projects for large & medium Manufacturing entities, trading houses, various product Exporters, Corporate administrative offices, Professionals and Chartered Accountants etc., like Financial Accounting Systems, Payroll Management Systems, Schedule Management Systems, Equipment History Management Systems, Textile Design Management Systems, Marketing Management Systems, Ticket Booking (Travels) Systems, Bus Collection Maintenance Systems etc.,

“FA – Financial Accounting” System was developed in the year 1991 by our venerated Founder Mr.T.Mani Sekaran Msc., (Electronics) with the Simple Accounting Methodology derived by our revered Chartered Accountant Mr.G.N.Jayaram F.C.A, one of the leading C.A. Professional in Coimbatore and Erode Region. It is a simple and very powerful accounting tool, till used by more than 200s of Auditors. Mr.K.P.Muthrasu one of the founder of Zigma Computers paved a great contribution for the development of the firm in myriad ways.

With their contribution and using their Basic Accounting System, in the year 1994 Mr.R.Manokar, with the five years of Corporate Experience, innovated the outstanding, State-of-the-art software Package – “Zigma Integrated Financial Accounting system Software (ZIGFAS)” by integrating the Inventory package with the existing Accounting system. The software was developed by using the Foxpro in a DOS Version. It was designed for the Manufacturing Industries and Trading houses and considerably enhances the quality of the major modules from purchase to end sale.

We proud to state that our team developed an embedded software for North Eastern Railway (Raipur) through a third party to drive their electronic controller through our software to test the Locomotive Engine’s Traction Generator and generate a TG Graph which states the current condition’s and performance status. In addition to that, we also developed a software through third party to Southern Railway (Hyderabad) which gather the Locomotive’s Governor’s conditions status through an electronic controller and generate Empty Load, Half Load, Full Load test etc. to test the Governor’s Conditions. Both software are running successfully for the past 7 to 9 years. Thus we develop software for any verticals, against the order.
